Welcome to our Project!

This is our website for ISM 6225, Distributed Information Systems. This website details crime in the United States using data from the Federal Bureau of Investigation and their API found on data.gov. Here we will display the total number of arrests within a year by the type of offense (Aggravated Assault, Larceny, DUI, etc.). It can be insightful to see which types of offenses count for the most arrests, as well as how those numbers have changed over the years.
